About Me

   ABOUT ME I am Wutthichai Chuenarrom, a Network Engineer with strong hands-on experience in ISP environments. I have over 2 years of experience working with FTTx, VLAN design and segmentation, Routing, Switching, and PPPoE. I am responsible for PPPoE server administration, integrating PPPoE with RADIUS authentication, and configuring OLT devices for customer access networks. I have experience in network monitoring using Zabbix, including creating separate monitoring VLANs to enhance system visibility and security. Additionally, I have designed and implemented VPN solutions, including Site-to-Site and Site-to-Client VPN, to support secure inter-site and remote connectivity. I work in a structured manner with a strong focus on clear network design and well-documented network diagrams prior to implementation.
